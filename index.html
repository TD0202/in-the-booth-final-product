Feature-rich website UI prototype

Photography made easier for new and returning customers.

McCall Southern is moving its small photography company into a modern digital experience. This prototype centralizes booking, sample galleries, print orders, touch-up requests, online payments, and customer support in one customer-facing website.

What the website includes

Built around the full customer journey

Portfolio Browsing

Sample galleries help customers preview graduation, marching band, headshot, and family photography before booking.

Online Ordering Flow

A mock shopping cart, payment page, and order confirmation show how customers could buy prints and digital downloads.

Touch-Up Requests

Customers can submit retouching details, attach notes, and request edits through a dedicated service form.

Ideas to support marketing

Designed to be a true asset to the business.

  • Seasonal promotion banner for graduation and marching band season
  • Testimonials section to build trust with new customers
  • Featured packages with clear pricing and benefits
  • Contact options for the main office and studio location
  • Mobile-friendly layout so future customers can browse on any device

Prototype Scope Note

This site demonstrates the complete user interface only. Buttons navigate between pages, forms display realistic fields, and sample content is populated throughout. Full back-end processing will be implemented later by web developers.
