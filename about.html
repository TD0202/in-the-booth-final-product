About the company

Why McCall Southern is building In The Booth

The Photography Company is a small photography business with 15 employees, a main office, and a small photography studio. As graduation requests, marching band orders, and miscellaneous photography jobs continue to increase, the company needs one central digital location to keep work organized and improve customer experience.

In The Booth was created as a front-facing website concept that helps the business keep up with future technology trends. The site brings together customer engagement, sample photos, booking flows, touch-up requests, and purchase paths in one modern experience.

Business Goal

Create a trendy and scalable digital presence that supports long-term growth and positions the company for future generations of customers.

Operational Goal

Reduce fragmented manual processes by centralizing information, requests, and customer communications into one organized website workflow.

In The Booth

A modern photography experience built to support graduation, marching band, studio, and family sessions.
